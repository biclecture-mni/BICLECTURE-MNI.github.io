FEINDEL BIC LECTURE SERIES



ABOUT

These lectures are named in honour of Dr. William Feindel (1918–2014), Former Director of the Neuro (1972–1984) and Founding Director of the BIC (1984–1988). Dr. Feindel’s presence and contribution will be greatly missed at our weekly Brain Imaging Lectures, where he almost always asked the final, most-germane question of the day: one that usually bridged the clinical and research realms, and that brought together the past, the present, and the future of brain science.

Unless specified otherwise, lectures are held on Mondays at 1:00 pm
In the de Grandpré Communications Centre of the Montreal Neurological Institute.
